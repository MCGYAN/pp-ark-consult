About

Client outcomes first.
Rigour throughout.

PP Ark Consult and Investment is a Ghanaian professional services firm based in Lartebiokorshie, Accra. We advise enterprises and SMEs on consulting engagements, financial accounting, taxation, and the operational controls that underpin sound governance.

Our approach is direct: clear scope, documented recommendations, and practical implementation support. We aim to strengthen your financial position and operating rhythm without unnecessary complexity.
